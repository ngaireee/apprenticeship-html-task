Meet Your Coach

Sam, your business coach, is here to support with your apprenticeship. You'll be seeing her monthly in your 121s. You can also reach out with any questions regarding portfolio, EPA or general apprenticeship support.

Sam Osborne

Role: Business Coach

Bio: Hello! I'm Sam and I'm the Software Development coach at JustIT. Being a coach has allowed me to merge my passions for software development and people development. I have worked as a developer in both the public and private sector. I love to cook, travel and spend time in the pub! I spend most of my time lazing around with my dogs, Winston and Reggie. I'm a huge advocate for education for all, no matter your gender, race, sexuality or socioeconomic status, especially in STEM subjects.

Style of Working:

  • I love meeting new people and forming relationships. It's the reason I love my job so much!
  • I value honesty. I'll challenge your thoughts just as much as I'd expect you to challenge mine.
  • I'm super organised and super disoarganised all at the same time. I have tons of notes and todo lists and plans but I still always seem to forget something!
  • I'm all in and will do anything I can to help you succeed. I'll give you my full support and time, and I just ask for the same level of effort in return.

Hobbies:

  • Travelling - I aim to visit a new country at least once a year
  • Food & Drink - A relaxed meal and a glass of my wine is my favourite way to spend an evening
  • Gaming - I play anything from FPS to MMORPGs to cosy games. I love BG3, FFXIV, Marvel Rivals and the Two Point games to name a few!
  • Socialising - I love spending time with my husband and our friends. I'm always out and about and love a drink, a good chat and a few games of shuffleboard or pool. I even have a full sized pool table in my kitchen!

Communication Style

I prefer open, informal communication. Feel free to reach out however works best for you, email, phone, Teams, or Slack. That said, I'm way quicker to respond on Teams and Slack, so if you want a speedy reply, that's where to find me!
